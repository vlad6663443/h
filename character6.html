Natural History

The Earth Guardian is an ancient elemental being, born from the very essence of the world itself when the first forests took root and the mountains reached skyward. For countless ages, this protector has maintained the delicate balance between civilization and the natural world.

Dwelling in the heart of the Great Forest, the Earth Guardian can command all aspects of nature - from the smallest flower to the mightiest oak, from gentle streams to devastating earthquakes. Their connection to the earth runs so deep that they can sense disturbances in the natural order from across continents.

Though peaceful by nature, the Earth Guardian becomes a formidable force when the natural world is threatened. They have been known to rally entire ecosystems to their cause, turning forests into armies and mountains into fortresses to protect the innocent and preserve the balance of life.

Natural Powers

Earthquake

Causes the ground to shake violently, creating fissures and dealing massive damage to all enemies while leaving allies unharmed.

Damage: Extreme Cooldown: 45s

Thorn Barrier

Summons a wall of thorny vines that blocks enemy movement and damages anyone who tries to pass through.

Duration: 20s Cooldown: 25s

Nature's Blessing

Channels the healing power of nature to restore health and remove debuffs from all allies in a wide area.

Healing: Very High Cooldown: 30s

Natural Artifacts

World Tree Branch

A living branch from the mythical World Tree that grows stronger with each passing season and seasons all spells with nature's power.

Ring of Seasons

An ancient ring that allows the wearer to harness the power of all four seasons simultaneously.

Heart of Gaia

A crystallized piece of the planet's core that provides unlimited connection to earth's energy.

Cloak of Leaves

A living cloak made from leaves that never wither, providing camouflage and protection in natural environments.
